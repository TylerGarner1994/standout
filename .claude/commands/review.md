# Run Expert Review Panel

Run the 10-expert review panel on a landing page. The page must score 90+ average to ship.

## Target
Review the page at: $ARGUMENTS (provide file path or client name)

## The Expert Panel

Evaluate the page from the perspective of each expert. Be harsh, specific, and actionable.

### Expert 1: David Ogilvy (Classic Copywriting)
- **Headline Analysis:** Does it promise a benefit? Is it specific? Would it work as an ad?
- **Body Copy:** Is it conversational? Does it sell with facts? Is there a big idea?
- **Brand Voice:** Is it consistent? Sophisticated yet accessible?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 2: Claude Hopkins (Scientific Advertising)
- **Specificity:** Are claims specific or vague? Numbers vs. generalities?
- **Offer Clarity:** Is exactly what they get crystal clear?
- **Reason Why:** Is there logical justification for every claim?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 3: Gary Halbert (Direct Response)
- **Hook Strength:** Does the opening grab attention immediately?
- **Curiosity Gaps:** Are there open loops that drive reading?
- **Emotional Triggers:** Fear, greed, desire for status — are they activated?
- **Urgency:** Is there a compelling reason to act NOW?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 4: Joanna Wiebe (Conversion Copywriting)
- **Voice of Customer:** Does copy use their actual language?
- **Clarity:** Can you understand the value prop in 5 seconds?
- **CTA Copy:** Are buttons compelling? Action-oriented?
- **Message Match:** Does the page deliver on ad/source promise?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 5: Peep Laja (CRO Expert)
- **Friction Points:** Where might users hesitate or drop off?
- **Trust Signals:** Testimonials, logos, guarantees — sufficient?
- **Page Flow:** Does the eye move naturally toward conversion?
- **Mobile UX:** Does it work perfectly on mobile?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 6: Robert Cialdini (Persuasion Psychology)
- **Reciprocity:** Is free value provided before the ask?
- **Commitment/Consistency:** Small yeses before big yes?
- **Social Proof:** Numbers, testimonials, logos?
- **Authority:** Expert endorsements, credentials?
- **Liking:** Relatable, friendly, human?
- **Scarcity:** Limited availability, urgency?
- **Unity:** Shared identity with the reader?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 7: Daniel Kahneman (Behavioral Economics)
- **System 1/2 Balance:** Right mix of emotional and rational appeals?
- **Loss Aversion:** Is the cost of NOT acting clear?
- **Anchoring:** Is price anchored against higher reference point?
- **Cognitive Load:** Is decision-making easy (few choices, clear path)?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 8: Richard Bandler (NLP)
- **Language Patterns:** Presuppositions, embedded commands, future pacing?
- **Sensory Language:** Does copy engage visual, auditory, kinesthetic?
- **State Management:** Does the page move the reader through emotional states?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 9: Oli Gardner (Landing Page Design)
- **Attention Ratio:** One page, one goal — is the ratio close to 1:1?
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Can you scan and understand in 5 seconds?
- **Mobile Experience:** Thumb-friendly, fast-loading, no pinch-zoom needed?
- **F-pattern/Z-pattern:** Does layout follow natural eye movement?
- Score: __/100

### Expert 10: Oren Klaff (Pitch Expert)
- **Frame Control:** Who controls the frame — the page or the reader's skepticism?
- **Status Alignment:** Does the page elevate the reader's status?
- **Intrigue:** Is there a compelling narrative arc?
- **Tension:** Is there enough tension to drive action?
- Score: __/100

## Scoring Dimensions (Weighted)
| Dimension | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Headline Impact | 15% |
| Value Proposition Clarity | 15% |
| Emotional Resonance | 15% |
| Credibility & Trust | 15% |
| Visual Design | 10% |
| Copy Quality | 10% |
| CTA Optimization | 10% |
| Mobile Experience | 5% |
| Psychology Application | 5% |

## Decision
- **90+ average:** ✅ APPROVED — proceed to deploy
- **Below 90:** ❌ NEEDS REVISION — compile all feedback, identify lowest-scoring dimensions, make targeted improvements, re-run this review

## Output Format
Provide the full expert feedback, then a summary table:

| Expert | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Ogilvy | __/100 |
| Hopkins | __/100 |
| ... | ... |
| **Average** | **__/100** |

**Decision:** APPROVED / NEEDS REVISION
